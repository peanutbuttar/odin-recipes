Brownie Recipe

Description

an incredibly delious brownie recipe that is perfect for deserts or get togethers where you want to impress your family

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Stir cocoa and baking soda together in a large bowl.
  3. Mix in boiling water and 1/3 cup oil until well blended and thickened.
  4. Stir in sugar, remaining 1/3 cup oil, and eggs. Mix in flour, vanilla, and salt just until flour is absorbed.
  5. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Let cool completely before cutting into 24 squares.
