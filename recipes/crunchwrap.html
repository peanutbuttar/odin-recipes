Crunchwrap Recipe

Description

A homemade vegetarian Crunchwrap recipe which is tastier and healthier and cheaper. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • Kidney beans
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Guacomole
  • Tostada
  • Guacomole
  • Optional: sour cream and salsa

Steps

  1. put kidney beans, cheese, pico de gallo, guacomole, lettuce, tostada on the tortilla
  2. wrap it from each side and encase it completely
  3. put on stove and warm up both sides until finished
  4. if you would like, in my opinion it tastes better with sour cream and salsa on the side whiile eating it
