Crunchwrap Recipe

Description

A homemade Crunchwrap recipe which is tastier and healthier and cheaper. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 burger buns
  • 2 Trader Joe's tikka masala patties
  • 1/2 sliced cucumber
  • 1 slice of pepper jack cheese
  • ketchup and mayo
  • 1/4 sliced onion

Steps

  1. put patties for stove for 10-15 minutes, flip until evenly done on both sides, put cheese on after first flip
  2. put ketchup and mayo mixture on the bottom buns
  3. put patty then cheese then cucumbers then onion and then top bun.
